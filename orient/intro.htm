Welcome to the Chemeketa Online Learning Orientation

Online learning is new to many of us, so you are not alone if you are wondering if online classes are right for you.

To help you in your decision making process, we have prepared an online orientation which will give you an idea of what it takes in order to be successful in online learning.

This Orientation will help you:

  • Figure out if your computer is adequate for online learning at Chemeketa
  • Decide whether your learning style and computer skills are well-matched to online learning
  • Become acquainted with how to log on and use eLearn (our online course software)
  • Discover various assistance options
