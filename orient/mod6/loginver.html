Verifying Your Username

 


Go to the following website:
https://banweb.pcc.edu/banweb_home.htm

Click on "Login to Secure Area"


Type in your User ID.
(this is your student ID number, usually your Social Security Number)



Type in your PIN number (unless you have changed the PIN Number, it is your six digit birth date. Your PIN is your birth date (in the form MMDDYY) or the number you chose if you have changed it. (If the day or month is a single digit, start it with a zero)


Click on the "Login" button.


You will have to re-enter your PIN Number.


Click on the "Login" button.

Click on "Personal Information"


Click on "View E-mail Address(s)"


Your eLearn User Name will be displayed under "Username for on-line courses E-Mail Address(es)"


Log in again using this correct user name and your password at this page:
http://www.distance.pcc.edu/login/


If you are still unable to login, please contact the WebCT Helpdesk at (503) 977-8222 or send them an e-mail at webct@pcc.edu
